Will Robot take my Job: The Age of AI

Many experts agree that in the next five to ten years a lot of vocations will be completely automated. A disturbance similar to the one farmers experienced during the Industrial Revolution would affect accountants, manufacturing employees, truckers, paralegals, and radiologists, to mention a few.

According to the video I watched, several trucks and cars, including Teslas, already use AIs. The self-driving automobiles and trucks are regarded as safe thanks to machine learning. Automated cranes also use technology and Ais, and they benefit from it.

In my opinion, artificial intelligence (AI) will eventually replace humans since they can work more effectively and quickly than we can.
