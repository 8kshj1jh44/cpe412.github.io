How AI is searching for Aliens: The Age of AI

For the first time, we have the technology to contribute to the solution to the issue of whether there is other intelligent species in this galaxy. With the development of artificial intelligence, scientists are once again searching for extraterrestrial life while simultaneously starting to question if a brand-new life form has emerged on earth. According to NASA officials, artificial intelligence may help in the search for extraterrestrial life and the identification of neighboring asteroids. According to a statement from NASA, the space agency plans to use artificial intelligence, or A.I., technologies like machine learning to understand data that will be gathered by future observatories like the James Webb Space Telescope or the Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS) project.

I became aware of how effective and valuable artificial intelligence may be after watching previous videos. I can argue that it has advanced significantly, from creating prosthetic hands and legs to finding life on other planets. Artificial intelligence is extremely advantageous to humanity. I believe that in the future, mankind will be heavily dependent on artificial intelligence.

Artificial intelligence should be viewed as a chance rather than a danger to society. If knowledge ended up in the wrong hands, it might be quite frightening.
